Hidden in Plain Sight

The assignment was to hide a ridiculously large object in a drawing in such a way that the viewer would not notice it for the first several second but once it does he or she must wonder how it had never been clear from the beginning.
We first had to draw our room and to draw a personal object (I chose my watch). Then you had to draw the object in the room, but in a size that would be ridiculous. Now the challenge was to hide the object in a confusing mess and draw the eye to another part of the drawing by making that part empty. Lines of sight help to divert the eye away from the object and onto the empty focus point, in my case the window.
