A short overview of where I have been:
I was born in 1980 from a true mother and an engineer, in Den Haag. Before high school I had lived in Curacao, Malaysia and Holland in between.
I spent high school in Teteringen (Breda).
After high school I went to study technical physics in Delft. I spent a long time trying to get trough university and after too many years decided to cut the corner and get my bachelors degree at the TH Rijswijk. During all this I developed serious skin trouble that I struggled with for years and that hospitalised me three times for a week or two. I mention this because it taught me so much.
After the physics story I had the opportunity to choose further education or a career. I felt a job as a physics engineer would give me premature grey hair and tried the Design Academy. They accepted me and I have been having a lot of fun trying to pass the different classes (much more fun than I had at the TU Delft)
