POK LIVE AT HOME

I had been playing around since high school with systems of letters, stacking them, calculating the shadows they cast. This kind of investigation was a kind of fascination to me.
My brother was mixing music at the time and had made a mix that he called ‘Pok Live at Home’. I used this title to draw the stack of words and the shadow. Then I wanted this drawing digitally. I could only work with MsPaint at the time, so that is what I did. Ms Paint can only work with separate pixels, lines, shapes and fills in one given colour at the time. So I basically drew this picture pixel by pixel.
I started with a basic isometric box, calculated by counting pixels, made a grid, cut out the letters and finally calculated the shadows by counting pixels again.
I have no idea why but I enjoyed this kind of work.
