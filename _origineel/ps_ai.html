Various Photoshop and Illustrator work

Various Photoshop and Illustrator work. Photoshop was the first I learned to work with. Up till than my only experience with computer graphics programs was with MsPaint.
In 2006 I took a week that I had free, installed Photoshop and sat down to see If I could learn to make it work. I set myself the task of making the Coen-logo (which might not be the best program to do this with, but I had no idea). After this I spent days playing with layers and filters and investigating different functions.
In the first year at the Academy we were taught Illustrator and with the experience with Photoshop I had gained at this point this went a lot quicker.
Since then I have used both a lot and am more and more combining their abilities for my assignments, depending on the program of the assignment.
